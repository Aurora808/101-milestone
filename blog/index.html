MY THOUGHTS

Sometimes I look at her and I see a dancer doing a pose with one foot on the ground under the dress. Other times I look at the painting and she is being pushed or falling. I find it fascinating that she can be both strong and fragile.

I was going to do a card of a bird, but that didn't turn out, so instead it's going to be a placard card with hearts. I'll cut the top half off. Adaptation can be the source of satisfaction.

I started putting height and weight instead of height and width! Working too long - time to take a break!

The hardest part of making art - knowing when to stop fussing with it!

Paragraphs have automatic margin on top and bottom. Need to set margin to 0 pixels to get rid of unwanted spacing and then adjust padding of element accordingly.

Painting is about going meditatively and paying attention to my gut. If it sings to me - I need to honour that - even if it wasn't what was originally planned. Make a plan, but be flexible to what the universe has in store for me.

Wow! Validating code is important. I see things can look good, but not work. There is such a victorious feeling when I see the green bar in the validator tool!

It's much better to take on a little bit of coding daily rather than taking large breaks of time in between learning.
