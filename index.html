MY PORTFOLIO

Creating & Experimenting

Offline creativity

In high school, I drew in black and white with charcoal, pen and pencil. The world of color was overwhelming at the time.

Last summer, I took the plunge and started to play with color in arcylics and watercolor. It's all about having fun, exploring different color combinations with a few realistic works now and then.

Check out my paintings, photography and whatever else I'm playing with these days.

Exploring & Learning

Online coding

After a few successful VB programming efforts to make my day job more efficient, I became more curious about coding. I've taken a few part-time college courses locally, attended Canada Learning Code workshops and recently I signed up for Skillcrush's Break Into Tech program to explore the world of tech in-depth.

So far, I love the mix of design and function in coding. These are website examples and exercises from my latest learning projects.
